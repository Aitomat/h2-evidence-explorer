Paste this where you want the explorer to appear. Please keep the source credit line.

<iframe src="https://h2medicine.org/widget/"
        style="width:100%;border:0;height:720px"
        title="H2 Evidence Explorer" loading="lazy"></iframe>
<p style="font-size:13px">Data: <a href="https://h2medicine.org/">H2Medicine</a></p>

German site? Use https://h2medizin.org/widget/. Want a single topic only? Append ?indication=respiratory, ?method=inhalation, ?ev=3, ?human=1, or ?titlelink=off. Full instructions & auto-height variant on the widget page and in the README.
